# VEHO Working & Remote Work Policy

**Effective Date: April 1, 2026**

These rules are defined to ensure fair and appropriate working practices for all members.

---

## ◇ Working Time

- Working hours: 8 hours  
  - Normal: 08:30–18:00 (Break: 12:00–13:30)  
  - Core time: 09:00–17:00  

- Example:
  - 08:00–17:00  
  - 09:00–18:00  

If working outside core time (arriving after 09:00 or leaving before 17:00), please report in the Slack channel [00_impress_veho_team]

---

## ◇ Break Time

- Over 6 consecutive working hours: minimum 30 minutes  
- Over 10 working hours per day: minimum 1 hour  
- Night work (22:00–06:00): minimum 45 minutes  

If it is difficult to take a break due to workload, please consult your PM  

---

## ◇ Work Location

Work must be performed at VEHO office  

---

## ◇ Late Arrival / Early Leave

Please report the following in advance to [00_impress_veho_team]:

- Date and time  
- Reason  

Repeated cases without valid reason may affect evaluation  

---

## ◇ Leave / Remote Work Application

- Please apply in advance via Slack  
- Remaining leave can be checked using the Slack command `/check`  
- Paid leave will be automatically deducted  

### ■ Half day

- /vehobot take off morning, YYYY-MM-DD, Reason  
- /vehobot take off afternoon, YYYY-MM-DD, Reason  

Two half-days count as one full day  

### ■ One day

- /vehobot take off, YYYY-MM-DD, Reason  
- /vehobot remote, YYYY-MM-DD, Reason  

### ■ Multiple days

- /vehobot take off, YYYY-MM-DD, YYYY-MM-DD, Reason  
- /vehobot remote, YYYY-MM-DD, YYYY-MM-DD, Reason  

---

## ◇ Paid Leave / Unpaid Leave

- Paid leave:
  - 12 days/year (after 1 year)  
  - 1 day/month (less than 1 year)  

- Special leave:
  - Own marriage: 3 days  
  - Child’s marriage: 1 day  
  - Death of parent / spouse / child: 3 days  
  - Death of adoptive parents (including parents-in-law): 3 days  

- Unpaid leave:
  - Death of grandparent / sibling: 1 day  
  - Marriage of parent / sibling: 1 day  

Same-day requests or unjustified leave may affect evaluation  

---

## ◇ Overtime / Holiday Work

Prior approval from PM is required  

---

## ◇ Remote Work Policy

### 1) Remote Work Frequency Limit

Remote work is allowed up to 2 days per month.

#### Important

If remote work exceeds 2 days without prior approval, the additional days must be taken as either Paid Time Off (PTO) or Unpaid Leave.

This rule is designed to protect employees by ensuring managers issue work requests appropriately. If you feel pressured to work unpaid overtime, please escalate to your manager or executive team.

---

### 2) Impact on Evaluation and Salary Increases

Exceeding the limit may negatively affect performance evaluation and annual salary increases.

---

### 3) Special Circumstances

If you need to exceed the limit, prior approval via Slack is required.

#### Approval flow

- Engineers: PM  
- PM: CEO  

#### Deadline

Requests must be submitted by 09:00 AM on the day.

#### Registration

Post confirmation to #0000_impress_veho_team  

#### Example

/vehobot remote, YYYY-MM-DD, Reason (approved by @Approver)

---

### 4) Sick Leave

If you are unwell, prioritize taking leave instead of working remotely.

---

## ◇ Compliance & Data Access

Please do not access any data without proper authorization or view others’ personal information without permission.  
These actions may violate company policies and affect trust within the team.  
If confirmed, violations may impact performance evaluations and may be subject to disciplinary action.  
If you notice any inappropriate behavior, please inform the PM. If the PM is involved, feel free to reach out directly to the CEO.

---

## ◇ Workplace Conduct

As the office is a shared space, please help keep it comfortable for everyone (both during working hours and lunch breaks):

- Keep your voice at a reasonable level  
- Avoid non-work-related conversations in the working area  
- Use headphones or keep the volume low when using phones/laptops  
- Be mindful of others and maintain respectful, professional communication  
- Avoid eating strong-smelling food in the workspace
